# Yomiru Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-25

Yomiru stores account data, reading status and source URLs so users can manage a synced personal library.

## Data We Collect

- Account profile: email, name and preferred language.
- Library metadata: source URLs, titles, chapters and reading status.
- App usage metadata required for authentication and synchronization.

## How We Use Data

- Authenticate users.
- Sync library metadata across sessions.
- Retrieve chapter content when requested by the user.

## Offline Data

- Offline downloads are stored locally on the user's device.
- Auto-cached content is removed after 24 hours.
- User-downloaded offline content persists until the user removes it or uninstalls the app.

## Account Deletion

Users can permanently delete their account from the app profile screen. Deleting an account removes synced server metadata associated with that account.

## Contact

Support and privacy contact information is provided in the store listing for the published app.
